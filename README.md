# RaceNGuilds

RaceNGuilds is a PaperMC plugin that combines **races and guilds into one system**.  
When a player joins the server, they choose a **race**, and that race becomes their **guild**.

Each race has unique **bonuses and drawbacks**, encouraging different playstyles, PvP strategies, and economic roles.

The plugin also includes **custom items** and **special TXT files** required for full functionality.

---

## Core Concept

- Players choose a **race** on start
- A race **is also a guild**
- Each race has:
  - strengths
  - weaknesses
  - unique mechanics
- Races are designed to be **asymmetric but balanced**
